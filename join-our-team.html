Careers at Valley Care

Valley Care is always looking for dedicated professionals who are passionate about providing excellent care. Join us in making a positive impact on people's lives.

Current Opportunities

We regularly hire for the following positions:

  • Care Coordinators
  • Licensed Care Providers
  • Case Managers
  • Administrative Assistants
  • Community Outreach Specialists
  • Clinical Supervisors

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary and benefits
  • Professional development opportunities
  • Supportive team environment
  • Flexible scheduling options
  • Ongoing training and certification support
  • Health insurance and retirement plans

Qualifications

Requirements vary by position, but generally include:

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Valid driver's license (for some positions)
  • Background check clearance
  • Professional certifications as required by position
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills
  • Commitment to quality care and ethics

How to Apply

Interested candidates should submit their resume and a cover letter explaining why you'd like to join Valley Care.
