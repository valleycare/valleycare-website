Service Boundaries

Understanding what we do and don't provide

Our Service Area

Valley Care operates within defined geographic and service boundaries to ensure we can provide the highest quality care to our clients.

Geographic Coverage

We currently serve the following regions:

  • Central Valley area
  • Surrounding counties (within 30 miles)
  • Select urban and rural areas

Service Limitations

To ensure quality and sustainability, we have the following boundaries:

  • We do not provide emergency medical services
  • Specialized psychiatric care may require referrals
  • Services available during business hours (with 24/7 emergency contact)
  • Minimum care plan commitments apply

Referral Services

If your needs fall outside our service boundaries, we'll help connect you with appropriate resources.
