Practical, social and wellbeing support – no personal care

Our Community Support service is unregulated. It focuses on companionship, practical help and routines that keep people independent, connected and confident at home.

What we support with

  • Companionship and conversation
  • Domestic tasks (cleaning, tidying, laundry)
  • Shopping and errands
  • Meal preparation (no feeding)
  • Routines and wellbeing
  • Walks, hobbies and community activities
  • Post‑discharge settling‑in

Who this service is for

People who don't need personal care, but do want a bit of extra help to stay organised, connected and well – including older adults, people with low‑level mental health needs, and families needing flexible support.

What Community Support does not include

  • Personal care (washing, dressing, toileting)
  • Medication administration
  • Mobility or transfers
  • Continence support

When those needs appear, we move people safely into our Regulated Care service.
