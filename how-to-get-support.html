How to Get Support

Getting started with Valley Care is easy

Getting Started

Follow these simple steps to connect with Valley Care services:

Step 1: Initial Contact

Reach out to us through phone, email, or our contact form. Tell us about your needs and we'll guide you through the next steps.

Step 2: Assessment

We'll schedule a comprehensive assessment to understand your specific needs and circumstances.

Step 3: Care Planning

Based on the assessment, we'll develop a personalized care plan tailored to your goals and preferences.

Step 4: Support Begins

Our team will begin providing services according to your care plan with regular check-ins and adjustments as needed.

What You'll Need

  • Valid identification
  • Insurance information (if applicable)
  • Medical history summary
  • Emergency contact information
  • List of current medications (if applicable)

Cost and Financial Options

Valley Care accepts multiple payment options including:

  • Insurance coverage (many plans accepted)
  • Medicare and Medicaid
  • Private pay
  • Sliding scale fees (based on income)
